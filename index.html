Dalgona Coffee Recipe Page

Dalgona coffee is a South Korean Coffee Recipe which became the frontline news during the lockdown. This might fascinate everyone since all the ingredients are in your cupboard.

Ingredients

  • Hot water
  • Instant coffee or expresso granules
  • Milk
  • Sugar

Dalgona Making Process

Step 1: Combine the instant coffee, sugar and hot water
Add 2 tablespoons of instant coffee, 2 tablespoons of sugar and 2 tablespoons of hotwater to medium mixing bowl


Step 2: Whisk, whisk, whisk until the coffee is light and fluffy!
Using an electric hand held mixer, whip the coffee is light and dusky brown in colour and fluffy


Step 3: Serve with milk
Use a spoon to garnish a glass of milk or hot or cold milk with the coffee cream. Serve with a straw, and stir, stir, stir to enjoy!
